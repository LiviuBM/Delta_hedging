Retail Hedge Advisory

Build a portfolio protection plan with delta-based sizing.

This app does not place trades. It evaluates your stock portfolio, compares hedge approaches, and recommends options to review based on your protection target, budget, and time horizon.

What the new version does

  • Looks at portfolio-level protection, not only one stock at a time.
  • Compares single-name puts with an index hedge when the portfolio is diversified.
  • Shows cost, estimated delta coverage, scenarios, and when to review the hedge.

For educational and advisory use only. Options involve risk and can lose value quickly.
